Why creators dread tax season

Multiple platforms, no withholding, and a spreadsheet that lies to you.

Income from everywhere

AdSense, Twitch payouts, Patreon, brand deals, affiliate links — nobody's adding it up in one place, so you never see the real number.

Nobody withholds for you

Unlike a job, no tax comes out automatically. Miss quarterly payments and the IRS adds penalties on top of the bill.

"Profit" that isn't real

The number in your account isn't yours — 25–35% of it belongs to taxes. Spending it is how creators end up owing thousands.

Questions

Is this tax advice? ▾

No — it's a planning estimate to help you set money aside so you're not blindsided. It uses 2025 federal brackets, the standard deduction, and self-employment tax. Always confirm with a tax professional before filing.

Do you store my income data? ▾

No. The calculator runs entirely in your browser — your numbers are never sent anywhere. Only your email (if you join the waitlist) is collected.

Why self-employment tax? ▾

As a creator you're self-employed, so you pay both halves of Social Security + Medicare (15.3%) on top of income tax. It's the part that surprises people most — and the main reason your "profit" is smaller than it looks.

What is CreatorLedger going to be? ▾

A financial back-office built for creators: connect your platforms, see real profit across all of them, auto-calculate your quarterly tax set-aside, and catch every deduction — without wrestling with QuickBooks. This calculator is a free preview. Join the waitlist to shape it and get early access.
